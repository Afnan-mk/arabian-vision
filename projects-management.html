01. Projects Management Services

Considering that 70% of projects failure are always related to poor project management process and knowledge. Therefore, Arabian Vision came up with a unique solution. We provide you the project management services as its real mean, no matter your project size or the project deliverables, you can rely on Arabian Vision with trust.


Our services included but not limited to:
  • Planning your project:
  • Budgeting and resource-allocation strategy:
  • Risk management:
  • Procurement management:
  • Quality management:
  • Communication management:

Project management as a concept depends on a revolved cycle of tasks, such as: planning, organizing, and controlling. The first principle of success is planning. This task includes the preparation of the following plans during the preconstruction stage: (Design, Time, Budgeting and allocating resources, Risk management and safety, Procurement, Quality, Project organization, communication..etc.)

In budgeting, our cost estimation is based on trusted and updated prices. In addition to that, we monitor and control the cost of each task during the project to avoid any extra costs. In resource-allocation, we provide a “portfolio” context for allocation decisions that incorporates the priority strategic, mission, and operational goals for the project. We define a disciplined and consistent framework for evaluation of all proposed uses of defined capital resources.

Risk management is a creative process that involves identifying, evaluating, and mitigating the impact of the risk event. Risk evaluation prioritizes the identified risks by the likelihood and the potential impact if the event happens. Risk mitigation is the development and deployment of a plan to avoid, transfer, share, and reduce project risk.

  • We monitor and manage the preparation of the tender documents to comply with the objectives of the project.

We identify the relevant quality standards of the project, and how to meet them in cooperation with the design consultant and the technical supervision team.

It is the process of monitoring and controlling communications throughout the entire project life cycle to ensure the information needs of the project stakeholders are met.
